About TilleValle

TilleValle is a family-owned farm dedicated to providing fresh, organic produce to our community. Our mission is to promote sustainable farming and healthy living.

We grow a variety of fruits, vegetables, and herbs using environmentally friendly practices. Our products are harvested with care and delivered fresh to your doorstep.

Our commitment extends beyond just farming; we actively engage with local farmers to support sustainable agriculture and fair trade practices. We believe in transparency, quality, and community empowerment.

Our farm is located in the lush region of Gilgil, where the climate and soil conditions are ideal for growing nutrient-rich produce. We use organic fertilizers and avoid harmful pesticides to ensure the health of our customers and the environment.

Thank you for supporting local agriculture and choosing TilleValle for your fresh farm goods. We look forward to serving you with the best nature has to offer.

Founded in 1985, TilleValle has grown from a small family plot to a thriving farm that supplies fresh produce to thousands of customers annually. Our history is rooted in tradition and innovation.

Our dedicated team of farmers and agricultural experts work tirelessly to ensure the highest quality standards. We invest in continuous training and sustainable technologies to improve our farming practices.

Sustainability is at the core of everything we do. We prioritize water conservation, soil health, and biodiversity to protect the environment for future generations.

We also engage with the local community through educational programs, farm tours, and partnerships with schools to promote awareness about healthy eating and sustainable agriculture.

At TilleValle, we believe that good food starts with good farming. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community makes us a trusted source for fresh, organic farm goods.
